MIGOS

This is an urban hiphop band located in the US. It is made up of three members:

  • Quavo
  • Offset
  • Takeoff

The band was formed in 2008 in Lawrenceville, Georgia. The band is managed by Coach k.
Migos released thier debut single "Versace" in 2013 and from then on it has been hit after hit.

Migos released their debut album 'Yung Rich Nation' in in July 2015 and their most recent contribution has been Culture_III.
